# Octavious Labs - Email Outreach Sequence

## Sequence Overview
- **Goal:** Get meeting with CTO/CIO/COO/CEO
- **Target:** Enterprise companies, 50-1000 employees, $25M+ revenue
- **Angle:** AI efficiency gains, cost savings (like the CA 2/3 ERP savings)

---

## Email 1: The Hook (Cold Outreach)

**Subject:** [Company Name] + 66% ERP savings?

**Body:**
Hi [First Name],

I came across [Company Name] and your leadership team - impressive growth.

I'm working with a founder who helped a company similar to yours save **2/3 on their ERP costs** through AI automation - $160k in realized savings.

Curious if you're exploring ways to optimize your technical stack heading into Q2?

Happy to share the details - no pitch, just a quick chat to see if there's alignment.

Best,
John

---

## Email 2: Follow Up (3 days later)

**Subject:** Following up - AI stack optimization

**Body:**
Hi [First Name],

Just circling back on my last note.

If AI efficiency isn't a priority right now, no worries - totally understand.

But if it's something you're thinking about, I'd love to show you what's possible.

Quick 15-min call?

Best,
John

## Industry-Specific Angles

### Law Firms
- **Hook:** "Reduce billable hour administrative work by X%"
- **Pain:** Document review, case research, client intake
- **Angle:** Free up attorneys to bill more

### Accounting Firms
- **Hook:** "Tax season efficiency - reclaim X hours per client"
- **Pain:** Manual data entry, compliance, client communication
- **Angle:** Do more with less during peak season

### Online Education
- **Hook:** "Scale from 1k to 10k students without adding headcount"
- **Pain:** Student support, content delivery, marketing
- **Angle:** Growth without proportional cost increase

### Insurance
- **Hook:** "Cut underwriting time by X%"
- **Pain:** Manual processing, compliance, policy management
- **Angle:** Faster quotes, more binding

### Financial Advisory
- **Hook:** "Compliance automation - reduce manual work by X%"
- **Pain:** Reporting, compliance documentation, client onboarding
- **Angle:** More time with clients, less paperwork

---

## Key Principles

1. **Short** - 5-6 sentences max
2. **Specific** - Use company details, not generic
3. **Value-first** - Give before you ask
4. **No pressure** - Leave the door open
5. **Test and iterate** - Track what works
